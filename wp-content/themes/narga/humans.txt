# Introdution

    NARGA WordPress theme was made by Nguyễn Đình Quân a.k.a Narga, an extremely versatile HTML5 & CSS3 WordPress Starter Theme based on ZURB Foundation, HTML5 Boilerplate, hNews microformat
    Because it's a percentage based grid system, it means that it will perfectly adapt to all screen resolutions (mobile, tablets and big screens).
